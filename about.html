About Me

I am a senior CS major with a passion for software development. I have experience as a web development intern, where I created websites and developed a Java programs. I also worked on an EHR system for a surgical clinic. My goal is to secure a job in the software field.

Background

I am currently a senior majoring in Computer Science, passionate about software development and eager to solve complex problems through technology. My academic journey has provided me with a solid foundation in computer science principles, algorithms, and data structures, which I have applied to various projects and internships.

During my undergraduate studies, I engaged in several hands-on projects that allowed me to enhance my coding skills and develop a keen understanding of various programming languages and technologies. I completed my internship as a Web Development Intern, where I had the opportunity to create dynamic websites and applications. This experience honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, particularly in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java.

Additionally, I contributed to the development of an Electronic Health Record (EHR) system for a surgical clinic, which involved improving system functionality and user experience. This project deepened my understanding of how technology can be leveraged to streamline complex processes and enhance operational efficiency.

My background also includes a solid understanding of version control systems, particularly Git, and a strong commitment to writing clean, maintainable code. I am continuously learning and staying updated with industry trends to ensure that my skills remain relevant and sharp.
